## Slide 3 — What Zaivo does

**One button. The job runs itself. The address remembers everything.**

1. **Renter presses a button** (a link/QR — no app, no account) and describes the issue.
2. **Zaivo prices it** from real completed jobs nearby — no quote-chasing.
3. **A matched, licence-verified tradie** who's online right now is dispatched — the *right* trade, automatically.
4. **The landlord's own rules** approve it (or it's one tap on their phone) — no chasing.
5. **Everyone watches one live screen**; the tradie can't mark it done without photo evidence.
6. **The renter/PM taps Verify → the tradie is paid same day.**
7. **It writes itself into the property's permanent record** — asset, warranty, certificate, price.

> Walk it once, fast. The "aha" is step 4 — approvals happen without your team
> in the loop.

## Slide 10 — "But what about…" (the honest FAQ)

| Their question | Straight answer |
|---|---|
| *Is it another system to learn?* | It replaces steps you already do manually. Value on the first job; nothing to migrate. |
| *Do my landlords lose control?* | The opposite — they get explicit rules + a full log. Money never moves without their rule or their tap. |
| *What if the price jumps on site?* | Small changes auto-apply and log; anything bigger **pauses the job** and asks the payer. A surprise bill can't happen. |
| *Are the tradies any good?* | Licence + insurance verified before they go online; every job scores their quote-accuracy. Bad operators sink. |
| *Who holds the money?* | Not us — a licensed payment provider (Stripe). We never touch client funds. |
| *Is the AI making decisions?* | AI proposes; deterministic rules and *humans* decide anything about money or compliance. AI outputs can be wrong and are disclaimed — a human is always in the loop on what matters. |
| *What does it cost my renters?* | Nothing, ever. |

> Have this slide ready but don't present it unprompted — pull it up when the
> question lands. Candour here is the differentiator.
